The race, explained

One format.
Eight stations.

HYROX is fitness racing: run one kilometre, complete one functional workout station, and repeat it eight times, indoors, against the clock. In Vancouver, that plays out December 18–20 at the Vancouver Convention Centre.

Ridge CrossFit & Athletics — Maple Ridge’s only CrossFit affiliate — trains every piece of that engine under one roof: the running, the strength, and the functional work between them, inside a room that pushes you to your best. Where community meets strength.
